The whole operation,
on one board

Signups, schedules, capacity, coaches and PT don't live in separate apps — in a gym they're one flow. Praxella models that flow end to end, so nothing falls between a WhatsApp message and an Excel tab again.

02 · Schedules, capacity & coaches

Every coach's day,
every room's hour

The timetable is the source of truth: recurring classes with rooms and coaches, live capacity on every session, and a per-coach board that makes double-booking impossible to miss. Change a class once — members, coaches and the front desk all see it.

  • Live "spots left" on every session — full is full
  • Coach calendars: classes and PT on the same lane
  • Completeness checks: no class goes live without a schedule, coach and fee
03 · Personal training

PT packages that
track themselves

Sell a package once — Praxella counts every session against it, books slots on the coach's real availability, and tells you when a member is due for renewal instead of letting the last session quietly slip by.

  • Packages by session count and validity window
  • Slots come from the coach board — no clashes with classes
  • Renewal surfaced before the package runs dry

Praxella brand kit

Everything above derives from this kit. The mark is a 3×3 session grid: four corner slots plus the center form the X of praxella — the coral center is "the booked slot", the one every gym runs on. It reads at favicon size and builds from plain rounded rects (trivial to reproduce as SVG in src/lib/brand.ts land).

The mark · session grid

On light · on dark · single-color fallback. Clear space = one tile. Favicon: the mark alone on Mat.
