Design System 2.0 — Phase 2 approval artifact

One product family, on every screen, in both languages, in both themes.

Decisions №1 and №2 are ✅ RULED (2026-07-20): the 5-tab IA per shell, pronounced icons (Option B), hide-on-scroll, and FIRST-CLASS DESKTOP for all three shells with full mobile/desktop parity. This v3 adds the staff shell (never visualized before) and the three first-class desktop vignettes. The binding text lives in docs/design-system/design-system-2.md; execution Waves 1–4 cite it section by section. How to say yes: ✅ the whole sheet · Decision №1: per-shell IA + icon Option A/B + hide-on-scroll yes/no · Decision №2: App Frame / First-class / Hybrid. Any "adjust" = one line back to the lane; the primitives absorb it.

Spec §2

The primitive kit

Six contracts every surface adopts: PageHeader, TabBar, StatusChip, EmptyState, Dialog, and the fmt module (dates/times/money/phones — always bidi-safe in Arabic). Shown in the light product palette; all token-backed, so dark comes free.

Buttons — role-true
Record payment Open diary View billing ⌫ Decline

Primary = brand (teal here, crimson at Proline). Destructive = outline + icon + confirm, never solid at rest. One radius (10), one height scale, visible focus ring.

StatusChip — one pill vocabulary
Active Paid Partially paid Pending Overdue Lapsed Expiring ≤7d Trial

One status → one chip → one place per card. “Partially paid” is new — the green-Paid-above-red-balance contradiction becomes unrepresentable. Domain→variant mapping lives in one vocabulary module.

Spec §3

Tab-bar IA — the footer menu, fixed

✅ RULED 2026-07-20 — Decision №1 approved in full

One shared bar: max 5 slots including More, 44pt targets, labels that never truncate (short Arabic label variants), active = color + top indicator, opaque surface, safe-area aware. Staff keeps its 4+More. Proposed folds below — each is a one-line config if you want a different five.

Portal: Home · Classes · Progress · Billing · More (PT + Profile fold in; PT clients get a Home card — closer than today)
Coach: Today · Attendance · Students · PT · More (Trials fold in; a “trial today” card surfaces on Today)

Icon treatment — per your note, Option B makes the icons the visual anchor. Both shown below; every screen on this sheet wears Option B.

Option A — compact (not chosen)✅ Option B — pronounced (24px icons, 56pt bar) · RULED

Hide on scroll — current best practice on both platforms: the bar slides away as you scroll down (content gets the full screen) and returns the instant you scroll up or reach the top. Material 3 ships this as standard scroll behavior, and iOS 26's own tab bars now minimize on scroll the same way. Our rules: reveal on any upward scroll, at the top, and whenever a sheet/keyboard opens; disabled for reduced-motion users; the More sheet always brings it back. Try it in the live frame below.

Spec §2–§3 applied

Re-imagined screens @390

Slim one-badge header with tenant identity · member content above promo · belt path as the emotional core · calm zeros · category colors · pronounced tab bars. Portal Home shown light + dark and en + ar; staff and coach Today follow.

Camera & status bar (best practice, shown in every frame): the app declares viewport-fit=cover and pads with env(safe-area-inset-top) — the header's surface extends up behind the system clock/battery and around the camera island, so the app owns the full screen while no content or title ever sits under the island. Bottom: the tab bar pads with env(safe-area-inset-bottom) so the home-indicator area belongs to the bar, not the content. This is the standard iOS/Android installed-app treatment and rides Wave 0's viewportFit fix.
